# From CCDC:
Clear description of the attack or incident
Scope of the attack – how many systems, applications, services have been
impacted.
Time window and sequence of the attack
Identification of the source for any attack
Document the delivery mechanism
Damage or monetary impact
Describe remediation methods
Provide supporting evidence, log files, diagnostics
Timeliness of reporting relative to the time of an incident is also a factor in assessing
